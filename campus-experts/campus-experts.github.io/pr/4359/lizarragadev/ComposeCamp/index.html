Compose Camp

Nov 21, 2022

Compose Camp 2022 is a training with two ‘tracks’: Beginner and Experienced, as well as a conference day in which there will be training in the creation of user interfaces for Android with Jetpack Compose.

Compose Camp is an activity inspired by JetBrains and Google at an international level, we will bring the experience of all the Compose Camps organized worldwide and in Spanish.

This event will take place online from November 21 to 26 at the hands of Kotlin La Paz. There will be excellent speakers, gifts, the best willingness to share with you and best of all: COMPLETELY FREE!

  • Day 1: Your first Android App and Building app UI
  • Day 2: Display lists and use Material Design and Navigation and App Architecture
  • Day 3: Connect to the Internet and Compose Essentials
  • Day 4: Layouts, theming and animation and Architecture and state
  • Day 5: Accesibility, testing and performance and Form factors.
  • Day 6:
    • Prototipado en Android con Firestore y Jetpack Compose
    • Navegación avanzada en Jetpack Compose
    • 10 cosas que debes saber de Jetpack Compose
    • Design Systems en Compose La guia practica
    • State Hoisting with Jetpack Compose
    • Compose Multiplatform Overview
    • Adaptabilidad y Accesibilidad con Jetpack Compose
    • The magic of IR
    • Compose Compiler & Runtime: Beyond Android
    • Migrate my app to Compose?…let’s do it!

The event will last 6 days, of which 5 will be specifically in an accelerated training of creating applications with Jetpack Compose and a day with conference.

Location: Livestream via Twitch and YouTube Date: December 21-26, 2022 Time: 19:00 UTC-4

You can find more info at Compose Camp 2022
