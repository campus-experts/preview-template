Expenses and Reimbursements

Requesting Reimbursement

All expenses must be approved before the event. Expenses not pre-approved will not be reimbursed. Refer to the sponsorship guidelines to see how to request a reimbursement

Onboarding to Payment Central

  • Head over to the campus-experts.github.ioopen in new window repo's issuesopen in new window page
  • Create a new issue, and select the 💰Onboarding Payment Central option.
  • Fill it out, and submit!
  • Check your email in a few days for an email from Microsoft Payment Central, and fill in all the required banking details.
  • If asked to fill the SSPA form ping @juanpflores to check with the Microsoft team. Important: Double-check that the bank account and details on the form are correct. We will send all payments to the account added to this form, and changes will require you to create a new account on Payment Central. If you need to update the bank or personal information, contact @juanpflores to start an update request.

Tracking your Expenses

You will need access to Campus Experts expenses folder on Google Drive, which can be found hereopen in new window. If you do not have access to this folder, ping @juanpflores on the GitHub Education Discord.

  • Ensure that you have opened an issue, and have been approved for monetary support
  • Save your receipts!
  • Create an invoice using this templateopen in new window
  • Create a new folder in the expenses driveopen in new window with the title: YOUR NAME - EVENT NAME - DATE OF EVENT
  • Upload your receipts to the newly created folder
  • Fill out the Expense Reporting Formopen in new window

Getting Paid - Campus Experts from the US

  • Create an invoice for the total amount, this invoice should include a list of all expenses and their amounts.
  • Include the address of GitHub HQ: GitHub Inc, 88 Colin P Kelly Jr Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, NORTH AMERICA (This does not mean that you need to physically mail your invoice in, but digital invoices must also be addressed here)
  • Invoices are paid in 30 days. In extreme cases, we can pay sooner, please talk to Juan Pa if this is necessary.
  • Email invoices@github.com, cc juanpflores@github.com with:
    • Your completed invoice.
    • Attach the receipts (note that this is in addition to uploading them to the Google Drive mentioned above).
  • Please set the subject line to: YOUR NAME - MM/DD/YY OF INVOICE - being-an-expert ISSUE NUMBER
  • For example, I upload a receipt for an Uber ride, for an event that I’ve opened an issue for. The event was on the 18th-19th March, and I took the Uber on the 18th. I send my invoice on the 20th of March, with a 30 day pay period. Juan P Flores - 03/20/17 - being-an-expert 44 is the issue number, you can find this next to the title of an issue or in the URL: for example https://github.com/campus-experts/being-an-expert/issues/4
